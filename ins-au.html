About Sevitti Investigations

Ethics are of paramount importance to our work. The private investigators making up Sevitti Investigations are all licensed and experienced in providing confidential, ethical, and unbiased results.
Our team is led by a Master Investigator and our head office is in the heart of Stoney Creek.

We are fully licensed and insured and can work anywhere so long as the case originates in Ontario. We are proud members of:

  • Council of Private Investigators of Ontario (CPIO)
  • World Association of Private Investigators (WAPI)
  • Stoney Creek BIA
  • Stoney Creek Chamber of Commerce

We offer competitive rates with a commitment to the truth, no matter the complexity of the case.
When you need the expertise and reliability of professionals contact Sevitti Investigations. With over 25 years of experience in the Investigation and Security industry, we have worked with a variety of clients from individuals and families to businesses and law firms. Our specialties include surveillance and research, and we have the knowledge, tools, and strategic partners to conduct your investigations and help you find the truth.

Our team

Our team consists of professional private investigators led by a Master Investigator with over 25 years of diverse investigative experience. All of our employees are licensed and adhere to our strict Code of Conduct and the Ministry of Community Safety and Correctional Services Code of Conduct.
