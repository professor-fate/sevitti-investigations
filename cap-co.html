GTA Commissioner of Oaths Services

Process Serving

When you need a sworn declaration affirming something to be true, you’ll need to do it in front of a commissioner of oaths. A commissioner of oaths is someone authorized by the Province of Ontario to witness specific sworn declarations.

It could be to affirm a declaration for applying to the government for something or by a company when they need information from you.

Sevitti Investigations has a sworn “commissioner of oaths” on staff to help. We offer professional, affordable and convenient service in the Hamilton, Niagara and Toronto areas.

Administering oaths for specific documents

Sevitti Investigations has a licensed Commissioner for Oaths authorized to verify affidavits of service and statutory declarations.

If you require a commissioner of oaths to verify your documents, please call or email to book a meeting so we can confirm your documents are within our power to verify.

  • Affidavits of service - statements in writing and signed under oath
  • Statutory declarations – written and signed statements of facts a person declares to be true
